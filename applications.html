Product Applications

Black Silicon Carbide Applications

Used for grinding non-ferrous metals, ceramics, glass, and stone. Ideal for cutting tools, sandblasting, and refractory materials. Black silicon carbide is also widely used in the production of abrasives, refractory materials, and ceramic products.

Brown Aluminum Oxide Applications

Suitable for grinding hardened steel, alloy steel, and high-speed steel. Commonly used in bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and blasting. Brown aluminum oxide is also used for surface preparation, deburring, and polishing of metal parts.

White Aluminum Oxide Applications

Perfect for precision grinding of tool steel, fine finishing, and polishing applications. Used in vitrified grinding wheels and resinoid bonds. White aluminum oxide is also used in the manufacture of sandpaper, grinding wheels, and other abrasive products.

Refractory Materials Applications

Used in furnaces, kilns, incinerators, reactors and other high-temperature processing vessels. Our refractory materials are designed to withstand extreme temperatures and harsh environments, making them ideal for steel, glass, cement, and petrochemical industries.
