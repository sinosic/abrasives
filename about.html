    Company Overview

    Founded in 2008, Star Ocean Industrial Limited has established itself as a leading manufacturer and global supplier of high-quality abrasive materials. With over 15 years of industry experience, we are committed to providing premium abrasive solutions that meet the highest international standards.

    Based in Lianyungang, Jiangsu Province - a strategic industrial hub in China - we leverage our prime location and advanced manufacturing capabilities to serve clients worldwide. Our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner in sectors.
