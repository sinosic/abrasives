Industrial Applications

Our products are used in a wide range of industrial applications, providing reliable solutions for various manufacturing processes.

Metalworking Industry

Our abrasives are widely used in metal fabrication, providing precise grinding, polishing, and surface finishing for various metal components.

  • Stainless steel polishing
  • Carbon steel grinding
  • Aluminum surface treatment

Ceramics Industry

Our refractory products provide essential heat resistance and structural integrity in ceramic kilns and manufacturing processes.

  • Kiln linings and components
  • Ceramic tile production
  • Advanced ceramic manufacturing

Automotive Industry

Our materials are used in various automotive manufacturing processes, from engine component production to surface finishing.

  • Engine part grinding
  • Automotive body polishing
  • Heat treatment furnaces

Aerospace Industry

Our high-precision abrasives and high-temperature refractories meet the stringent requirements of the aerospace industry.

  • Turbine component finishing
  • Aerospace alloy processing
  • High-temperature furnace linings
