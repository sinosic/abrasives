Welcome to Star Ocean Industrial Limited

Star Ocean Industrial Limited is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality abrasive materials with over 15 years of industry experience. Based in Lianyungang, Jiangsu Province, we specialize in producing premium abrasive products that meet the highest international standards.

Our comprehensive product range includes Black Silicon Carbide, Brown Aluminum Oxide, White Aluminum Oxide, and specialized Refractory Materials. These products are essential components in various industrial applications including metalworking, construction, automotive, and manufacturing sectors.
